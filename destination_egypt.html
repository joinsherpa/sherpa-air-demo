Egypt

About Egypt

Egypt is a country located in northeastern Africa, known for its rich history and iconic landmarks. It is home to the world-famous pyramids, the Sphinx, and the Nile River, the longest river in Africa.


Things to do

  • Explore the Pyramids of Giza
  • Visit the Valley of the Kings in Luxor
  • Cruise the Nile River
  • Discover the Temples of Abu Simbel
  • Explore the Egyptian Museum in Cairo
